Adopt a Pet Today!



Each year, thousands of animals are stranded on the street without a home or loving family. Adopting an animal can change the life of not only the animal, but of the adopted family as well.

Consider adopting an animal from the local pound or Humane Society this holiday season instead of shopping at a pet store. Often times, adopted animals have a lot of love to give and just haven't had the chance to share it with a caring family.

The benefits of adopting an animal through the Humane Society go beyond supporting a single pet. Adoption fees help support the Humane Society conduct important work such as;

  • preventing animal cruelty
  • ensuring health and longevity of farm animals
  • rescuing pets from poverty
  • advocating for animal rights in research
  • securing continued tranquility of wildlife
